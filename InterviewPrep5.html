What are CSS combinators?

Combinators combine the selectors to provide them a useful relationship and the position of content in the document. This comes in handy when you want to style certain elements all at once rather than styling elements individually. There are 4 CSS combinators: 1. Descendant Selector, 2. Child Selector, 3. Adjacent Sibling Selector, 4. General Sibling Selector
